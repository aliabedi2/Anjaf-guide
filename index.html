Imam Ali bin Abi Talib

In the center of the old city of Najaf is located the pure shrine of the Commander of the Faithful, Imam Ali bin Abi Talib, peace be upon him, and it is the most important landmark in the city.

Kufa Mosque

The Kufa Mosque is one of the mosques to which travelers are often drawn, and some narrations reported that it was a temple to the angels before the creation of the Prophet Adam and then a temple to him.

check now

Al-Sahla Mosque

It is considered one of the oldest and most honorable mosques after the Kufa Mosque. It was built in the first Hijri century, and historical sources indicate that it was once a home for the Prophet Idris.

check now

Al Hanana Mosque

It is one of the honorable mosques, with an area of about 7,400 square meters, in the middle of which is the shrine that honored the head of Imam Hussein, peace be upon him

komil bin Ziyad

Komil bin Ziyad Al-Nakha'i is considered a great companion who spent 18 years in the life of the Messenger of God, and he is one of the companions of Imam Ali bin Abi Talib.

Fatimid Mosque

Lady Fatima Al-Zahra Mosque, adjacent to the shrine of Imam Ali bin Abi Talib, with an area of 61,000 square meters, is one of the most important religious landmarks.
